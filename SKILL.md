## Core Philosophy

### From Rendering to Photographing
The strategic transition from "rendering a scene" to "photographing one" is the defining threshold between amateur AI generation and high-stakes Visual Engineering.

### The Physics-First Mindset
While amateur approaches rely on subjective trial-and-error using terms like "cinematic" or "hyperrealistic," the professional Architect treats the AI as a physics simulator.

### Quiet Luxury
True authority is not signaled through loud branding, but through the understated precision of technical execution.
